about me

I am an interdisciplinary designer who specializes in architectural, graphic, and interactive design. I approach a project with an integrative mindset, seeing where one part of my knowledge can help supplement the other in new ways.

Growing up in the small town of Crested Butte, Colorado, I understand the importance of a strong community. My work is meant to bring people together, give space for interaction, and weave vibrant life into lifeless spaces. Living is better when we do it together, exploring and creating on both small and large scales.

My process is sketching, rethinking, perfecting, moving parts around, then going back and scribbling some more. I believe in progress through process, and each project feels like a scrapbook of items that when finally pieced together, it all fits.
